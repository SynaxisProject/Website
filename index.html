About Us

Starting with several film projects, that aspire to compete with highest quality of independent cinematography, we hope to portray the beauty and riches of the Orthodox Church. Leveraging the recent democratization of professional production quality tools from hardware to software we believe this well within the reach of our team. We operate as a loosely organized group of people that collaborate to realize the individual projects.

While remaining well within the Orthodox mainstream, we try to serve all christians and seekers by making the splendor of the Church more accessible through modern media.
